Corporate Compliance

In India, the Companies Act, 1956 acts as the foundation for all company operations. This act outlines the legal framework governing how companies are formed, financed, function, and eventually dissolve. To ensure transparency and accountability, the Companies Act mandates that companies comply with various regulations specified throughout its different sections.
